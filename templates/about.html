ABOUT US

Heart disease is one of the biggest causes of morbidity and mortality among the population of the world.

Prediction of cardiovascular disease is regarded as one of the most important subjects in the section of clinical data analysis.

The amount of data in the healthcare industry is huge. Data mining turns the large collection of raw healthcare data into information that can help to make informed decisions and predictions.

The aim of this project is to summarize some of the current research on predicting heart diseases using various combinations of machine learning algorithms and conclude which technique(s) are effective and efficient. Also, some future directions on prediction systems have been addressed.
